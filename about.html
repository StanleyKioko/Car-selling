Our Story

Blobe Stano Cars has been serving the Kenyan automotive industry for over a decade. We started with a simple mission: to make quality vehicles accessible to everyone across Kenya.

From our humble beginnings in Nairobi, we have grown to become one of the most trusted car dealerships in the country, with satisfied customers from Mombasa to Kisumu and everywhere in between.

We specialize in both new and pre-owned vehicles, ensuring that every car meets our strict quality standards before it reaches our customers.
